In order to sucessfully invoke "run_wrlog.bat" and "run_javadoc.bat" you must:

a) have Java 1.4.0 or higher installed on your machine
b) have WrDoc.jar installed on your machine (not free of charge)
c) have environment viariable WRDOC_HOME pointing to directory containing WrDoc.jar
   (alternatively, you may adjust paths in both batch scripts)
